Journal Guidlines
Good things:
  • Python learning: Do tutorials, build your own projects, or read books.
  • Fitness: Do pushups etc. exercises in the basement.
  • Guitar: Play the guitar, do tutorials, or read about it.
  • Read Books: Including fiction, non-fiction, work related, hobby related..
  • Housework: Clean up, fix things around the house, or read about it.
Bad Things:
  • Video Games: Eliminate video games.
  • Youtube/Netflix: Limit watching tv, videos to 15-30 min per day, eventually reduce to 0.
Habits to build:
  • Control yourself: When you catch yourself doing bad things, switch to good ones.
  • Daily Log: Log things throughout the day. Quickly review and log before going to bed.
  • Weekly Review: Review how the week has been add a journal entry summarizing the week.
